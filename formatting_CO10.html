Favorite Ice Cream Flavors

This is about some of my favorite flavors

My first flavor is strawberry

  • I like this flavor a lot and I get it from the market all the time

Another flavor that I like is half baked by Ben and Jerry's Ice Cream

  • It is vanilla and chocolate swirled ice cream with cookie dough bits and brownie chunks in it
  • It is really good because I like cookie dough and brownies and when you mix this all up with ice cream it taste even better

My Last favorite flavor is something called "All Nighter"

  • I get this ice cream from UD's Creamery. It is one of their custom flavors that the food science major students have created
  • It is coffee ice cream with cookie dough, chocolate fudge and toffee mixed in
  • This is such a good flavor and they also have lots of unique flavors at their creamery
